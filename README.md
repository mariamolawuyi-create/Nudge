# Nudge
### Nudging Economics Students Towards Clarity

**Live Demo:** [https://mariamolawuyi-create.github.io/Nudge] 

---

## What is Nudge?

Nudge is a career navigation tool built specifically for economics students in Nigeria.

Every economics student is told their degree opens every door. Nobody tells you which one to walk through. Nudge fixes that — it asks you 7 simple questions about your interests, working style, and goals, then matches you to the career path that actually fits you.

The name comes from behavioural economics — the idea that people make better decisions not when they are forced, but when they are gently guided. That is exactly what this tool does.

---

## The Problem

Economics students in Nigeria often graduate without a clear sense of what career path to pursue. Some are confused about their options. Others have a career in mind but don't realise their degree can take them there. Beyond confusion, many students also lack exposure to the specific tools and platforms — certifications, virtual internships, and online courses — that could help them build relevant skills and stand out.

The internet exists, but awareness of resources like Forage and Coursera remains low.

This affects students at every level:
- **First years** miss the chance to get a head start
- **Second and third years** don't know what internships to pursue or what skills to build
- **Final years** face graduation with a broad degree and no clear direction

---

## What Nudge Does

- Asks **7 simple questions** covering thinking style, motivation, work preferences, risk appetite, economics interests, sector preference, and certifications
- Matches you to **1 of 10 real career paths** available to economics graduates in Nigeria
- Tells you **why the career fits you** based on your specific answers
- Gives you **4 skills to build** beyond your degree
- Recommends **specific certifications** to pursue
- Provides **one concrete next step** you can take this month
- Links to **YouTube videos** explaining the career
- Links to **free virtual internships on Forage** to get practical experience
- Lets you **email your full result** to yourself for future reference
- Lets you **share your result** with friends

---

## Challenges

The original plan was to use an AI model (Anthropic Claude) to generate fully personalised career results. This was attempted using three different APIs — Anthropic, Google Gemini, and Groq — but each had quota or authentication issues that could not be resolved before the deadline. The career matching logic was built manually instead, using a scoring system based on research. AI integration remains a top priority for the next version.
